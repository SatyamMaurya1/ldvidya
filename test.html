Admission Information

Step 1: Online Registration

The online registration can be done by using this link. Ensure that you are using Internet Explorer 7 or above while registering your child online.

  • All fields marked with * are mandatory.
  • Parents with existing Parent ID and password must log in.
  • Ensure names and dates of birth match the birth certificate or Aadhaar card.
  • Incomplete forms will be rejected.
Age Criteria for AY 2025-2026:
Grade Date of Birth (From) Date of Birth (To) Age
Nursery 01-04-2021 31-03-2022 3 to 4 years
Grade 9 01-04-2010 31-03-2011 14 to 15 years

Step 2: Entry Level Test

Details of the entry-level test will be shared after registration. Below are the test subjects and their durations:

Grade Subject Max Marks Duration
LKG to 1 To be announced - -
2 to 3 English, Mathematics, Hindi 20 each 1 hour each

Step 3: Confirmation of Registration

  • A non-refundable registration fee of 10% of the annual tuition fees will be collected.
  • Submit the following documents:
    • Birth Certificate
    • Aadhaar Card (Masked Version)
    • Previous class report card (except Nursery)
    • Vaccination record
    • Passport-size photos

Step 4: Fee Payment

Tuition, book, and transport fees must be paid in installments:

  • First installment: Before 22nd March
  • Second installment: Before 22nd June
  • Third installment: Before 22nd September
  • Fourth installment: Before 22nd December

Examinations and Promotions

  • Annual promotions are based on continuous assessment.
  • Minimum 40% marks in each subject and 50% aggregate are required for promotion.
  • No re-examination will be conducted.
