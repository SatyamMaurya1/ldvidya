  • Parents with existing Parent ID and password must log in.
  • Ensure names and dates of birth match the birth certificate or Aadhaar card.

Step 3: Confirmation of Registration

  • A nonrefundable registration fee of 10% of the annual Tuition fees will be collected on confirmation of the admission. document for admission.
  • Upon confirmation of the admission , the following document should be submitted :
    • Copy of birth certificate of the child (Admission for nursery to class 1)
    • Copy of valid ID of the child ( like AADHAAR CARD)
    • Photo ID proof of parents. (like AADHAAR CARD)
    • Previous class report card (except Nursery)
    • Vaccination record
    • Four passport size photos of child.
    • Passport size photos of parents with child.
    • Transfer certificate.
    • PEN(Permanent Education Number) UDISE.
    • Blood group report of the child.
  1. TC is not required for nursery admission , If the joining at the beginning of the academic year.
  2. Student coming from outside of India should attest their TC by the education officer of the state/District/ Area from where the certificate is obtained . The seal and signature of the Education officer has to be verified by that embassy in India and Indian foreign affairs ministry .
  3. If the student is locally transferred within India the PEN number. and mark sheet from previous school has to be submitted.

Step 4: Fee Payment

Once document are submitted and accepted by the school office parents may proceed to pay the Tuition fees , Book fee and transport fee. Fee receipt will be issued by the school office.

  • First installment: Before 22nd March
  • Second installment: Before 22nd June
  • Third installment: Before 22nd September
  • Fourth installment: Before 22nd December

Step 5:

Collection of book and uniforms from the school store by submitting the fee payment receipt. Details of sibling discount can be obtained from the school office.

Examinations and Promotions

  • The Annual promotions are decided through a system of continuous assessment throughout the year .Marks obtained in the annual examination alone will not determine the promotion of the student. The total marks are divided among class tests, Terminal Examinations and regular class works and assignments.
  • If student does not show steady and consistent progress in studies, he/she is not liable to be promoted.
  • There will be no re â€“examination or examination out of schedule.
  • For being promoted a student must secure a minimum of 40 % marks in every subject and 50% in aggregate .
  • No leave except on medical grounds will be sanctioned during the examinations. Medical leave is to be supported by Medical Certificate. However, it is not a substitute for examination . The overall performance of the student will be considered.
  • A pass grade in value Education , SUPW , physical Education and Art craft will be an essential requirement for promotions.
  • All cases of doubt regarding valuation of answer papers and promotion rest entirely at the discretion of the principal.
  • A student who is unsuccessful in the same class for two consecutive years will automatically be disqualified from the continuance of studies in the school.
