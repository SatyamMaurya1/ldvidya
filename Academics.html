School Uniform Guide

Class NLU

Boys: White half shirt and check pants, black shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Girls: White half shirt, check skirt, black shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Classes I to V

Boys: White half shirt, maroon pants, black shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Girls: White half shirt, maroon skirt, black shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Class VI to VIII

Boys: Pink check half shirt, gray pants, black shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Girls: White full kurti, gray salwar, black shoes and white shocks with three stripes.

House Uniform

Boys: White T-shirt, white trousers, white canvas shoes, white shocks with three stripes.

Girls: White T-shirt, white trousers, white canvas shoes, white shocks with three stripes.
